The idea

Every building has a story. Most of it is being lost.

Which unit's heating is temperamental. The plumber who actually knows the risers. What broke last winter, what it cost, and how it finally got fixed. Today that lives in people's heads, group chats and a drawer of receipts — and it walks out the door the day someone leaves.

AirXperience turns it into the building's institutional memory — written down the moment it happens, kept for good, and worth more every year you run it. Turnover stops being a reset.
